What is my story? Let's see some details about me.

In 2022 I pressed the reset button on my career. In the last 5 years I was a Business Analyst, but since 2022 october I'm learning to become a graphic designer.

At first I've learned how to use Adobe Illustrator, Figma and Photoshop and after that I started design websites. At this point I've started to learn how to build static websites with html / css / vanilla javascript. In addition, I learned to use a 3D modeling software - Blender. I tried to figure out what I love to do.

In the following I show you what I can do as a designer and after that you can check some of my works.
